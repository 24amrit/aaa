About Us

Established in 2005, AAA UPS Enterprises Private Limited has earned a reputation as one of India’s top suppliers of reliable energy solutions. We specialize in products from trusted brands like APC (UPS, batteries, racks, etc.), Amaze (inverters, batteries), and Delta, offering only the best in quality and dependability.

Our head office is located in Secunderabad (Telangana), and in 2015, we expanded our presence by opening our branch office in Bhubaneswar (Odisha) under the name Savitri Electro IT Pvt Ltd.

At AAA UPS Enterprises, we take pride in providing energy solutions that meet the highest standards of performance and reliability. With our extensive experience and commitment to excellence, we are here to power your homes and businesses with products you can trust.
